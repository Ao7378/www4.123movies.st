Christmas Comes to Willow Creek

Ray and Pete are brothers who are truckers. Their personalities contrast each other. Pete is the responsible one, while Ray is wild. Al the man they work for who is also a friend of their father's, thinks he can finally get Ray to straighten up but it requires him to go to Willow Creek, a town in Alaska on Christmas. But to make sure he gets there, Pete needs to go with him. And the two of them don't get along. And Pete's son who blames him for his mother leaving them is forced to go with them. So they make their way to Willow Creek but Ray and Pete's son makes problems. And along the way they run into Jessie, a girl Pete asked out but when they went on a double date with Ray, she gravitated to him. And as usual Ray messed it up.
