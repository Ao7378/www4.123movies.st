Snooki & JWOWW 1

An inside look of reality show personalities Nicole 'Snooki' Polizzi and husband Jionni LaValle, Jenni 'JWOWW' Farley and Fiancé Roger Matthews as they live their lives together as best friends, and also reality stars. Nicole and Jenni are best friends and their signifcant others have also become friends through the years. Experience their lives from friends living together in Season 1, to being either a mother of two (Nicole) or a new mom (Jenni) in Season 4, the finale season. See how the two are ablt to balence their 'BFF' relationship, their own relationships, and family life. Watch how their lives have changed from what they were in the years of "Jersey Shore." The show has four season, and the fourth season will be the last, it premiered on June 21st, 2012, and its final season premired on November 5th, 2014. Recording of the fourth and final season continues into november 2014, with recording starting a few weeks before the birth of Jenni ''JWOWW' and Roger's child, Meilani Alexandra Matthews on July 13th, 2014. The fourth season also features events like Nicole 'Snooki' and Jionni's birth of a second child, Giovanna Marie LaValle, younger sister of Lorenzo Dominic LaValle born on August 26th, 2012, and Nicole 'Snooki' and Jionni's Bachelor/bachelorette parties in Miami, and also their wedding, which was on November 29th, 2014. In the first season Nicole 'Snooki' and Jenni 'JWOWW' shared an apartment on Mercer Street in Jersey City, New Jersey. Season two brought both back to where they usually live and was all about the birth of Lorenzo and Nicole 'Snooki' and Jionni becoming new parents, and Jenni 'JWOWW' and Roger taking more steps to make a stronger relationship while also thinking about their future. Season three brings the two closer with Nicole 'Snooki' and Jionni renting a house on the Jersey Shore just minutes from where Jenni 'JWOWW' and Roger live. It showed Nicole 'Snooki' and Jionni getting used to parenthood as Lorenzo was almost a year old at the time, and Jenni 'JWOWW' and Roger fixing the issues in their relationship and being to think about starting a family in the future, and including adoption as a very likely choice for the couple. Season four has brought Besties Nicole 'Snooki' and Jenni 'JWOWW' to live right next to each other in Manchester, New Jersey. Nicole 'Snooki' and Jionni rented a house next door to Jenni 'JWOWW' and Rogers house, but have sinced moved into their brand new house in northern New Jersey. Snooki and JWOWW airs on Wednesday nights at 10:00/9:00c on MTV.
