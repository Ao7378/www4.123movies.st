Breakfast with Scot

In Toronto, the Maple Leaf hockey player Eric McNally breaks his shoulder in four pieces while training and is forced to end his career. Five years later, he is a sportscaster, working with his assistant Nula that is the only person in the TV that knows that Eric is gay and lives with the lawyer Sam. When the drug addicted girlfriend Julie of Sam's brother Billy dies from an overdose, her son Scot is sent to the social care. Sam contacts Billy that is in Brazil, and tells that Julie has assigned him with the custody of the boy and the insurance money. Billy is tempted by the money and promises to return to Toronto in the end of the year and Sam convinces Eric to lodge Scot at home. Scot was raised by his mother and is a sissy. The lives of Eric and Sam turn upside down with the boy and Eric gives his best efforts to give a father's influence to Scot.
