The Avengers 7

John Steed works for British Intelligence and works with various partners, notably: Ian Hendry (series one), Cathy Gale (series two and three), Emma Peel (series four and five), and Tara King (series six). Steed is always the ultimate in culture and grace as he saves the world each week.
