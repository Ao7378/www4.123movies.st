The Tribe 2

In the wake of a deadly virus that has wiped out the adult population, the children of the world must now survive on their own. The sophisticated hi-tech society created by their forefathers has collapsed into anarchy, confusion and fear. It is in this new dangerous new world where the tribes must construct a new culture in their own image and learn that in the aftermath of disaster, there comes fresh opportunity and new responsibility. The future is theirs' to create.
