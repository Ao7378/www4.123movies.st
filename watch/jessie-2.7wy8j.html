Jessie 2

Jessie Prescott (Debby Ryan) is a 19-year old teen from Texas who moves to the one area where she can actually become someone special- New York city. There, she lands into a job as a nanny to a married couple's kids, who are both movie directors. Left with no other choice, she accepts the offer and finds herself with 5 penthouse owners. Her troubles start with pretty, fashion-addicted, 13-year old, Emma Ross (Peyton List), freckle-faced but handsome player, 12-year old Luke Ross (Cameron Boyce), lizard-loving, Hindu outcast, 10-year old Ravi Ross (Karen Brar), chubby the bear and pony loving, 7-year old Zuri Ross (Skai Jackson), and lazy, sarcastic butler, Bertram (Kevin Chamberlin). Jessie eventually learns to care for the new children, while somehow, start dating the hotel doorman, Tony
