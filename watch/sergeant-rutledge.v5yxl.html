Sergeant Rutledge

Circumstantial evidence suggests a first sergeant in a black cavalry regiment has raped and murdered a white girl and then killed his commanding officer. When he makes the case look even worse by deserting, an old lieutenant friend is sent to get him back - and also finds himself quelling an Apache uprising. The lieutenant then volunteers to defend the sergeant in court, where the full story starts slowly to emerge.
