Un Noël magique

Able carpenter-contractor Scott Terrell returns to his ancestral township Gifford to restore the gazebo-kiosk built by his grandfather as mayor. The municipal executive supposed to supervise him, widow Lindsey Monroe, cares only for the timing in view of an impending festival, Scott is determined to get every detail right, so he waits for missing original plans or specifications. In the mean time, he volunteers restore an antique desk new to the Monroe house. Lindsey's reluctance to celebrate Christmas as her later husband loved to is finally overcome by teen daughter Hannah with help from Scott and Lindsey's meddling Donna Chastain, both of whom who also help Hannah adopt and replace a lost puppy and match Scott with Lindsey, while the mayor gets to date and win back Donna in the eventful process. Somehow a Christmas stocking which Hannah got from a mysterious crone at a seasonal market seems to magically nudge the family time and again.
